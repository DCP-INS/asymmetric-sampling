the folder contains the following files :
-all_shafts_ba_pa:
it corresponds to the ITPC spectrum of all shafts from all electrodes implanted in left and right aac during syllables presentation
it starts with shaft from the left hemisphere(pa then ba) and follows with shaft from the right hemisphere (pa then ba) 
-all_shafts_ba_pa_individual_limits:
it represents the respective indexes of the boundaries between subjects (aka : number of shafts for each individual subject)
in this case there are 12 subjects * 2 hemispheres * 2 conditions (pa/ba) = 48 indexes
